GAME DETAILS

Venue

Sukkur IBA University Kandhkot Campus

Schedule

Feb 17, 2026

Details

  • Players must participate in pairs (two persons per team).
  • Each match will be played between two teams.
  • The winner team will get a prize.

Game Rules

  • A player must roll a six to move a token out of the starting area.
  • Rolling a six gives the player an extra turn.
  • If a player’s token lands on an opponent’s token, the opponent’s token will be sent back to the starting area.
  • Safe zones marked on the board cannot be used to eliminate tokens.
  • Players must move their tokens according to the exact number rolled on the dice.
  • The team that successfully moves all their tokens to the home area first will be declared the winner.

Registration & Fees

Entry Fee: RS. 200/- Per Team

Open to all departments of Sukkur IBA Kandhkot Campus.
