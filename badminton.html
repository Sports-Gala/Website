GAME DETAILS

Venue

Sukkur IBA University Kandhkot Campus

Schedule

Feb 17, 2026

Details

  • Players must register in pairs.
  • The winning pair will be awarded a prize.

Game Rules

  • Two pairs will compete in each match.
  • If the shuttle drops three times in a team’s court area, that team will be eliminated and the opposing team will qualify for the next round.
  • The final match will determine one winning pair.
  • Double hits are not allowed.
  • Picking up or hitting the shuttle after it touches the ground is not allowed.

Registration & Fees

Entry Fee: RS. 200/- Per Pair

Open to all departments of Sukkur IBA Kandhkot Campus.
