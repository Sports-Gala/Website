GAME DETAILS

Venue

Sukkur IBA University Kandhkot Campus

Schedule

Feb 17, 2026

Details

  • Players must register Individually.
  • Matches will be played in singles format (all vs all).
  • The winner will get a prize.

Game Rules

  • Chairs will be arranged in a circle, one less than the total number of players.
  • Music will be played while participants walk around the chairs.
  • Participants must not touch the chairs while the music is playing.
  • When the music stops, participants must quickly sit on a chair.
  • The participant who fails to get a chair will be eliminated from the round.
  • One chair will be removed after each round.
  • The last remaining participant will be declared the winner.
  • Pushing, pulling, or unfair behavior is strictly not allowed.
  • The referee’s decision will be final.

Registration & Fees

Entry Fee: RS. 30/- Individual

Open to all departments of Sukkur IBA Kandhkot Campus.
